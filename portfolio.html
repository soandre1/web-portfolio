When: December 2016 - May 2017

Contribution: Front-end developer and UI designer

Description: Those who are bedridden experience discomfort as a result of their prolonged recumbent position. The Piano Mattress is a current research project at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County developed by Ph.D. candidate Kavita Krishnaswamy which tries to solve this problem by using a mattress comprised of adjustable air chambers. Through BEDBOT’s web or mobile app interface the user can adjust the pressure of a single air chamber or a group of related air chambers. The user’s heart rate, blood oxygen saturation, and body temperature data is collected from sensors present on the Piano Mattress and displayed using graphs. A user's doctor can view their vital sign data as well as adjust the air pressure settings of their Piano Mattress.
